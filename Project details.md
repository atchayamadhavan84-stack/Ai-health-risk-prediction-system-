PROJECT TITLE

AI Health Risk Prediction System

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The AI Health Risk Prediction System is a web-based application that uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze patient health data and predict potential health risks. The system helps users identify possible diseases or health conditions at an early stage based on factors such as age, blood pressure, blood sugar, BMI, and lifestyle habits.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Many health problems are identified only after symptoms become severe. Manual health assessments can be time-consuming and may not always provide early warnings. Therefore, an AI-based Health Risk Prediction System is required to analyze health data and predict risks quickly and accurately.

PROJECT OBJECTIVE

Main Objective:

To develop an AI-based system that predicts health risks using patient health information and provides early warnings.

Specific Objectives:

1. Collect patient health data.
2. Analyze health parameters using AI algorithms.
3. Predict possible health risks.
4. Display risk levels instantly.
5. Assist in early disease detection.
6. Improve healthcare decision-making.

FEATURES

1. Patient Registration
2. Health Data Collection
3. AI-Based Risk Prediction
4. Disease Risk Analysis
5. Instant Result Display
6. User-Friendly Interface

INPUT PARAMETERS

1. Patient Name
2. Age
3. Gender
4. Blood Pressure
5. Blood Sugar Level
6. BMI (Body Mass Index)
7. Heart Rate
8. Lifestyle Habits

OUTPUT

Low Risk

If the patient's health parameters are within normal ranges.

High Risk

If the patient's health parameters indicate potential health concerns.
